Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg

  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. First bring out cold last night rice
  2. Turn up heat on stove to medium
  3. Dump chopped garlic in
  4. Stir fry aromatics
  5. Chop up and throw in some spam
  6. Drump rice in
  7. Pour mixed egg in after rice is cooked
  8. Add soy sauce for flavor
